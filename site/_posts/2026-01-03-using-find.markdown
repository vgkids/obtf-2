A simple Find utility (Command-F) let's you jump to sections of your file.

As you type, matches get more specific. Here, typing for `light` gives us results for times I mentioned Light, Lightning, and Light Bulb.
![OBTF-2 Screenshot displaying a search for Light, with matches Light (Prism), Light Bulb, and Lightning]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/obtf-2-find-light.png)

I can use the up/down keys to jump to that match:
![OBTF-2 Screenshot displaying a searching for Light, with the 2nd match selected-- Lightning]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/obtf-2-find-light-2.png)
